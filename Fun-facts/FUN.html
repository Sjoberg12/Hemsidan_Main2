About F1

Formula 1 is the highest class of motorsport racing for single seaters. The races are called Grand Prix and the pitstops are the fastest in any motorsport. This website is a collection of historic tracks, cars, drivers and more.

History

F1 started in the year 1946, where they started with open wheel racing. The first ever Grand Prix was the 1946 Turin Grand Prix. There where conflicts between racing and WWII, but after some time they went back and formed what we call Formula 1 today.

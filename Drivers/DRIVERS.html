Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton is one of the most iconic drivers of our time. Hamilton was born January 7, 1985 in Stevenage located in the United Kingdom. He started his F1 career in 2007 and won his first title in 2008 with Mclaren. Hamilton has won 7 championships and has tied with Michael Schumacher. His current team is Mercedes and his teammate is Valterri Bottas.

Michael Schumacher

Michael Schumacher is a retired German racing driver who has raced in Formula 1 since 1991-2006 and 2010-2012 seasons. Schumacher has won 7 Driver championships and has driven for Benetton, Ferrari and Mercedes. In December 2013, Shumacher suffered a severe injury which sent him into a coma untl June 2014. He is still rehabing from his injury, but if we put his injury aside, he is still one of the greatest of all time.

Ayrton Senna

Ayrton Senna da Silva was a Brazilian racing driver who was born in Sao Paulo, Brazil. He was active in the sport since 1984-1994 and he drove for Toleman, Lotus, Mclaren and Williams. He is most iconic with Mclaren, winning all three Championships with Mclaren and with his iconic Mclaren MP4/4. He died in a crash in Imola 1994 and his death will always be in our hearts.

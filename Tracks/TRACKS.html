Monza

The Italian grand prix is the fifth oldest national Grand Prix in the sport. The Grand Prix has been held every Formula One World Championship season since the sport started. It's a high speed track with a total of 11 turns and a lenght of around 5800 kilometers. This track is a classic for F1 fans.

Spa-Francorchamps

Spa-Francorchamps is a famous Grand Prix located in Belgium, Spa. The track is famous for its high speeds and high intensity start up to Eau Rouge. The tracks length is 14.9km and has 25 turns. The F1 cars achieve speeds such as 360km/h at the first straight. We want to honour the death of Anthoine Hubert who died in a fatal crash in 2019.

W07 Hybrid

The Mercedes F1 W07 Hybrid was one of the most succesful Mercedes F1 cars ever produced. It was Lewis Hamilton and Nico Rosberg which drove this car in the 2016 season. This car scored a whopping 765 constructor championship points and got 19 wins, 20 pole positions and 33 podiums. It had a win percentage of 90,47%. Nico Rosberg won the drivers cahmpionship against Hamilton with only 5 points between each other.

Ferrari F2004

The Ferrari F004 is a highly succesful Formula 1 car that was designed and produced by Ferrari for the 2004 season. This car won 15 out of 20 total races and scored 12 pole positions. The drivers were Michael Schumacher and Rubens Barichello. This car had a 75% win percentage but when it das driven by Schumacher it was transformed into a different beast

Mclaren MP4/4

The McLaren MP4/4 is one of the most successful Formula 1 car produced. Powered by a Honda engine and both Alain Prost and Ayrton Senna, there was no stopping it. The car competed in the 1988 Formula 1 season and had a 93.75 win percentage, winning 15 out of 16 races. The only race they didn't finish first was in Monza, which Prost retired in and Senna only finishing in 10th place
